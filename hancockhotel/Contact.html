GETTING HERE

HANCOCK Hotel is just a 55-minute drive from Bandaranaike International Airport. Or take the train for an authentic and fun-filled Sri Lankan experience. Kalutara South Train Station is only 5 km from the resort and is well connected to many destinations, including the capital, Colombo

HOTEL TRANSFERS

For travellers flying into Colombo, Hotel hancock is less than an hour away along a scenic route of lush lagoons, waterways and rural countryside. For a seamless transfer between the airport and the hotel, hotel hancock offers a luxury chauffeur service. Please provide your arrival and departure information at least 48 hours in advance. Simply contact us at hancock@hotels.com.
