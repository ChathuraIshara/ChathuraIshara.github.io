Where is your company at in the research journey?


We view research as an ongoing process of iteration, improvement, evaluation, and progress. We’ve designed our research offerings as a progression from a Sandbox study, suited for anyone with an idea and a basic prototype to a Correlational study designed for companies that are ready for an ESSA III study. Each study systematically elevates the feedback and data from real school communities, helping you build a more effective product that’s loved by schools.

FAQs



  • WHAT OBLIGATIONS WOULD I HAVE AS A PILOT COMPANY?

  • You must provide your solution to your school partner(s) at zero cost for the duration of the academic year, be available for and responsive to product feedback, troubleshooting, and modification, and attend codesign research meetings with the Leanlab team and the pilot school partner. You must also pay the agreed upon fees for service to cover the cost of the research study.

  • WHAT IS THE TIME COMMITMENT DURING THE RESEARCH PERIOD?

  • Companies will have 2-3 codesign meetings with the Leanlab research team to establish the research plan. If technology integration with the school partner is necessary, companies will work with the school's IT team to set that up. Companies that provide training for teachers or other end users would also be expected to give the training at the school's convenience. Once the study begins, companies are on "stand-by" for technological trouble-shooting.

  • CRITERIA FOR ACCEPTANCE -- HOW WILL LEANLAB SELECT SOLUTIONS?

  • We will be selecting solutions based on 4 criteria.

    • Stage:Does the company have the necessary capacity to pilot necessary capacity to pilot a MVP across multiple end users and contexts and participate in the iterative process of research and design? Does the company have the ability to quickly enhance their solution to meet user needs?
    • Research Foundation:Is the solution based in discovery/foundational research? What is the solution’s theory of change? Is there a clear research question?
    • School Needs:Is the solution aligned to the needs of our pilot school partners?
    • Values Alignment: Is the company aligned toLeanlab core values ?

  • WHAT OBLIGATIONS WOULD I HAVE AS A PILOT COMPANY?

  • Our priority is helping you achieve the accurate research results possible that help you enhance your product. For companies that are responsive to school community voice and that demonstrate increased value or positive impact on school communities, we will help connect you and facilitate warm introductions to schools in our network that have pain points aligned to your solution.
