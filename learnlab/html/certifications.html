Codesign Product Certification



Codesign Product Certification is open to all companies that complete a research study with Leanlab Education and make product modifications based on user feedback. The purpose of this certification is to assess a company’s ongoing commitment to the values of codesign.

Learn More ➤




Vision



Provide a trust mark in the education ecosystem that allows edtech companies to signal their commitment to developing their products with, not for, the education community.






What is Codesign?

Codesign is based on the simple idea that the "end-users" (students, teachers, parents) in education are the people with the expertise necessary to improve research design and education products. When we codesign, we engage in a collaborative process that unites critical insights from school communities with the expertise of researchers and product developers. The end result is that we make better products for school communities.
